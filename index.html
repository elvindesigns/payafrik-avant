While valuation models differ, payafrik SPV has chosen to value Ripple Labs (at least in part) based on the market capitalization of XRP, its digital token.  Ripple Labs itself provides access to XRP market capitalization at https://ripple.com/xrp/market-performance/. What is not included in this valuation is the impact of the licensing/subscription revenue Ripple Labs is bringing in as a result of its licensing model or the critical mass of financial institutions it has amassed over the course of the last year. While many draw a distinction between Enterprise Value (EV) and Market Capitalization (Market Cap), each measures a company’s market value.  The key to notes is that the terms are not interchangeable. Market Cap is the most simplified way to calculate a company’s size, value and growth/risk outlook, measuring the value of a company based on stock price alone.  EV, on the other hand, arguable creates a more accurate value of a company because it takes into consideration its debt obligations. While EV gives a more comprehensive view of a company’s worth, we have elected to keep the valuation as simple as possible.
